##Description
Daizy is a trending WordPress theme with lots of powerful features, instantly giving a professional look to your online presence. Daizy comes with everything you could ever need to build an amazing website, and it's perfect for any type of business! View the demo of Daizy Premium https://demo.speciatheme.com/pro/?theme=daizy

=== Frequently Asked Questions ===
1. Get Support - https://specia.ticksy.com
2. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
3. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
4. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
